How it works

Approximately 90% of everything that a person does is habitual – however, statistics say that one can successfully overcome a habit in three weeks time. Combining this mentality with the fear of flying, an anxious flyer will typically begin to “worry” about an upcoming flight in the weeks leading up to a flight.

The Flight21™ Online Curriculum by FlyHome will have you working for the 21 days (Three Weeks) leading up to the day of your flight, to assist you in re-programing your mind, and changing how your body and mind would typically feel prior to a flight.
